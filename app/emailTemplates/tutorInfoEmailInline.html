Thanks for signing up as a Volunteer Tutor!
Before you can start tutoring you'll need to go through a quick verification process.
To get verified, you'll need to record a short video or audio interview containing the following:
  • A brief introduction of yourself,
  • Why you want to be a Volunteer Tutor in our program, and
  • A quick tutoring demo, where you choose a concept from your respective field/subject/interest area and attempt to explain it as simply and as structured as you can, as if you were explaining to a tutee.
Email your interview to vteuwa@gmail.com with your name and student number in the subject field. (Please don't send it as a reply to this email, as this account is not monitored and your email won't be seen.)
A team member will review your interview and contact you with any further instructions.
Thanks again for applying to Volunteer Tutor Exchange!
